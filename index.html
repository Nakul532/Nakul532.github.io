01. The Profile

I fix what's broken before anyone notices it's slow.

Five years of cutting cycle times, building dashboards no one asked for but everyone uses, and translating between teams that don't speak each other's language.

07. Research

Published work.

Peer-reviewed research that bridges machine learning with healthcare impact.

§
Peer-reviewed · NMIMS · Published

Probability of Heart Disease Using Various Machine Learning Algorithms

Comparative study using the Framingham Heart Study dataset (4,241 patients, 16 variables) to predict 10-year coronary heart disease risk. Pioneered Boruta feature selection and SMOTE class balancing. Random Forest achieved 83.96% accuracy, outperforming Logistic Regression (66.69%), SVM, Naive Bayes, and KNN across accuracy, F1 score, and AUC. Identified age and systolic blood pressure as the strongest predictive features.
