01. The Pitch

I sit at the intersection of Business and Tech.

Most people are either great at building products or great at selling them. I sit at the intersection.

With a foundation in Embedded Systems, Machine Learning, and Robotics, I understand the technical constraints of a build. But my true passion lies in Product Strategy and Business Analysis, ensuring that what we build actually solves a $1M+ problem.
